OTAN SPOTTER

by Raffelberger Jungs

Orangutan has been endangered and their population has been dwindling in recent years. An alarming 2018 study that found the island of Borneo alone lost an estimated 148,500 orangutans between 1999 to 2015. Moreover, natural or artificial disaster such as forest fires often threatens their safety.

Mission : Developing reliable AI solutions as a service for nature conservation, biodiversity maintenance, and life environment analysis. Vision : Transparency, openness, and fairness for a better a world where nature, wildlife, and human beings live in harmony.

Our future commitment will include cooperation with growing key partners to realize the drone to become more ubiquituous at an affordable rate and gathering of dataset to be more comprehensive.
